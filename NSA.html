Programmes Associate of Science in Network Systems Development

Associate of Science in Network Systems Development

Programme Aims

This programme aims to provide students with sound practical knowledge of computing fundamentals, a thorough understanding of ethical issues, and management and planning skills that are associated with the computing profession. It develops students' knowledge and skills for the administration of computer systems and networks in a business setting.


Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this programme, students should be able to:


Use a range of tools, programming languages, methodologies, and quantitative skills to undertake computer systems development work;

Apply appropriate knowledge and skills and work responsibly in the design, installation, and configuration of secure computer systems and networks;

Admission Requirement

TGeneral Entrance Requirement for Associate Degree Programes


OR

A recognized diploma/higher certificate in a relevant field


OR

Reaches the age of 23 on September 1 in the year of admission with satisfactory work experience.

Further Studies and Career Prospects

The capability and diversity of our graduates have gained acceptance on credit transfer to more Bachelor's degree programmes and many graduates manage to receive multiple admission offers from different local universities for their degree study. In recent years, graduates were admitted to BEng (Hons) in Computer Science of the HKUST; BSc (Hons) in Computer Science and BEng (Hons) in Information Engineering of the CUHK; BEng (Hons) in Information Engineering, BSc (Hons) in Computer Science and BEng (Hons) in Computer Engineering in the CityU.
