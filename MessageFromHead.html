About Message from head

Message from the head - Dr. Cyril TSE, Acting Head

The Division of Applied Science and Technology (AST) offers academic programmes in various fields of science and technology which enable the younger generation to experience the application of different technologies and help them prepare for further studies and career.

Factitilities

Supported by experienced academic staff, we plan our curricula to provide students with the basic principles, concepts and methods of applications in their chosen areas of study. Our facilities include state-of-the-art computer laboratories and equipment, network and multimedia laboratories.

Acticities

We organize internship, field trips and joint student projects with various organizations, as well as visits and overseas exchanges to Japan, Singapore and elsewhere. Seminars, career talks, competitions and social gatherings are regularly held to enhance whole person development and peer learning among students.
I wish you every success in your study and believe that your study experience with us would be both enjoyable and productive.
