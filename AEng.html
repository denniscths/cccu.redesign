Programmes Associate of Engineering

Associate of Engineering

Programme Aims

The Associate of Engineering programme (AE) is a generic programme in engineering designed to match regional manpower requirements for graduates with broad-based engineering skills, provide a foundation for further studies.


This programme is an entry-level programme to the field of engineering. It provides a balance of theoretical studies and practical training to prepare students for a career in a broad range of engineering industries.

Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this programme, students should be able to:


Apply mathematical methods and analytical skills, and engineering methods and tools, to basic engineering design problems and derive solutions;

Produce digital and analogue circuits and software according to system specifications and standards;

Examine important ethical, socio-political, cultural and/or economic issues, affecting engineering in the local, national, regional and international contexts;

Admission Requirement

TGeneral Entrance Requirement for Associate Degree Programes


OR

A recognized diploma/higher certificate in a relevant field


OR

Reaches the age of 23 on September 1 in the year of admission with satisfactory work experience.

Programme Curriculum

Programme Curriculum 2015/16 Cohort

The core courses feature an appropriate combination of (1) Electronics and Electrical Engineering, (2) Computer and Communications Engineering, (3) Applied Mathematics and Statistics, and (4) Language and General Education.

Further Studies and Career Prospects

The programme is designed to facilitate credit transfer or admission with advance standing to degree programmes offered by both local and overseas universities. Graduates have been accepted by all local universities offering suitable engineering programmes. Recent examples include the degree programmes offered by the CityU such as the BEng (Hons) in Computer Engineering, BEng (Hons) in Information Engineering, the BEng (Hons) in Computer Engineering and the BEng (Hons) in Electronic Engineering offered by the HKUST, the BEng (Hons) Information Engineering, BEng (Hons) Computer Science and BEng (Hons) Computer Engineering offered by the CUHK.
