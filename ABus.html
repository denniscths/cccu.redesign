Programmes Associate of Science in Business Analysis

Associate of Science in Business Analysis

Programme Aims

The programme aims to equip students with a sound education and training in the applications of statistical principles and techniques and computing technology to business and industry.


Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this programme, students will be able to:


Apply mathematical and statistical skills to formulate and solve business problems;

Use a range of statistical and information technology applications to solve business problems;

Admission Requirement

TGeneral Entrance Requirement for Associate Degree Programes


OR

A recognized diploma/higher certificate in a relevant field


OR

Reaches the age of 23 on September 1 in the year of admission with satisfactory work experience.

Programme Curriculum

Programme Curriculum 2015/16 Cohort

Statisticians work with theoretical and applied statistics in the private and public sectors. The core of that work is to measure, interpret, and describe the world. It is common to combine statistical knowledge with expertise in other subjects. The programme features an appropriate combination of (1) Mathematics and Applied Statistics, (2) Information Technology, (3) Business Studies, and (4) Language and General Education

Further Studies and Career Prospects

Graduates can apply for the Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Computing Mathematics, and the Bachelor of Business Administration (Honours) in Managerial Statistics, City University of Hong Kong. They can also apply for the Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Statistics and Computing and Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Investment Science offered by Polytechnic University of Hong Kong.
